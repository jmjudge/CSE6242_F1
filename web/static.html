History of F1 Races

There are many rules in Formula 1 that each team ("constructor") must abide by. They change every year, but every now and then, the FIA makes significant adjustments to the rules. The 2014 season was one such major overhaul, making it difficult to accurately compare against seasons prior.

Below is an interactive line chart that displays the cumulative points that each of the top 10 drivers earned throughout a given season. We invite you to play around with our tool. Simply select the season you are interested in exploring and the data will refresh automatically. Additionally, you may hover over different data points to get a glimpse of a specific driver's performance throughout that season and race. And finally, to have an easier-to-view experience, you can filter the chart to show only specific drivers by clicking on a driver's name in the legend on the righthand side.
