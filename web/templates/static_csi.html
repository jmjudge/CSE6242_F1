Championship Sensitivity Index (CSI) by Season

About CSI

The Championship Sensitivity Index (CSI) measures how volatile a season's championship outcome was. Lower CSI values indicate more stable seasons where the champion was likely to win.

CSI = 1 - (champion's win probability)

Values range from 0 (perfect stability) to 1 (maximum instability).
