What if...?

Have you ever wondered how things would change if the racers made different choices? With our simulations, we bring that power to you. Simply specify a race and adjust your parameters to the desired settings. Then, we'll re-run that race thousands of times using a Monte-Carlo simulator to see just how drastically a race can change. You can add up to 3 races.
